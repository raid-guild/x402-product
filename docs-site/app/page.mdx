## Project Boards

The x402 product suite is managed by the following project boards:

- [x402 product](https://github.com/orgs/raid-guild/projects/31)

## Documentation

This repository includes research artifacts and project-specific proposals:

- **`artifacts/`**: research artifacts that inform proposals (RIPs)
- **`proposals/`**: proposals (RIPs) specific to the x402 product suite

> *Note: The official Raid Guild Improvement Proposals (RIPs) are posted to [RIPs](https://github.com/raid-guild/RIPs) and managed by [RIP Requests](https://github.com/orgs/raid-guild/projects/16). The proposals within this repository are either drafts or copies specific to the x402 product suite.*
